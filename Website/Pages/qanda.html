Q&A

Recon Ops

General

To play Recon Ops locate a download button, launch the launcher, sign up, Have Fun!

Recon Ops utilizes the clasic controls for a FPS shoot but theese can be changed in settings.

To report a bug you can contact us at our email, use in game suppport or post on our reddit.

Yes you can play with your friends by adding the in the friends menu in the lobby and joing the room the they create.

You can support the game by reporting bugs, sharing it with friends or on social media. We higly encourage making youtube videos about it or fan content.

Updates

All updates are free in the BETA program. There may be a one time price at Recon Op's launch but once youve paid it will be free forever.

We aim to patch bugs as they are discovered which results in small random updates. Big content updates take months to devlop so dont expect a lot of updates.

If playing on pc the launcher will automaticly patch the game when there is a update posted to our servers. If on mobile check your App/Play Store. *The game will force you to play on the latest version.

Devlopment

Yes we are still working on the game while its in it's BETA phase

We plan to continue to add content and features until its 1.0 release.

Platforms

As of the BETA you can only play on Windows, but MAC and Linux are coming soon. So are Andriod and IOS

The game is optimized for pc so for Win, Lin and Mac you should have quite steady gameplay but for Mobile and WebGL we recomend playing on lower graphics settings

We aim to keep the game the same across all builds but mobile may have lower res textures and less maps.

Accounts

No an account is free to make, all you need is a active email adress

We will try to implment accounts into games where its nessacary and most will connect to our main database.

We plan to implment that in the future but as of now, no.
