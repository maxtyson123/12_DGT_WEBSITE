SunnyLand

General

To play Sunny Land locate a download button, launch the launcher, sign up, Have Fun!

Use WASD, or Arrow Keys to move the player on keyboard, esc to open the pause menu in-game, controler movement is underdevelopment with joystick to move and y to jump.

To report a bug you can contact us using email.

Yes you can play with your friends by creating a multiplayer lobby and they join it.

You can support the game by reporting bugs, sharing it with friends or on social media.

Updates

The game is completly free to play, meaing you wont need to pay to join in on the fun.

Game Development takes time so updates occur once or twice a month. Multiplayer updates are very anoyying to implement so dont expect a lot of those.

If your playing on pc the launcher will automaticly patch the game when there is a update posted to our servers. If on mobile check your App/Play Store. *Multiplayer requires the latest version.

Devlopment

Yes its still underdevelopment, however once version 2.0 is released with the launch of level creator there will be less frequent updates

I plan to continue to add content and features until its 2.0 release.

Platforms

As of now you can only play on Windows, but MAC and Linux are coming soon. So are Andriod and IOS. WebGL demo also exists

The game is highly optimisized so most modern devices should run the game steadly

The game wont change across platforms, however in the demo there is a limit to how much you can play.

Accounts

No an account is free to make, all you need is a active email adress

I will try to implment accounts into games where its nessacary and most will connect to our main database.

I plan to implment that in the future but as of now, no.
