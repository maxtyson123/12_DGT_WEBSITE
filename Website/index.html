Sunnyland




Sunnyland is a peaceful 2d platformer. Play as a fox and explore multiple levels, collect gems, berries and dodge enimies like frogs and eagles. Join your friends in multiplayer races or speedrun through the main levels against the clock.

Questions And Awnsers

Sunnyland is currently only avlible for windows via its launcher. But we are working on bringing it to console via WebGl on Edge. Mobile develolpment is also underway and may soon be published to the google play store.

Sunnyland is free to play with possible cosmetics that may cost in the future

In Sunnyland you can expect many features that you would find in your other popular Platformer games such as Life system, Levels, Enimies, and much more. You can read more about the game HERE and about up coming features HERE

You progressed is saved locally, and can be reset in the settings menu. Previous levels can be replayed through the level select system.
