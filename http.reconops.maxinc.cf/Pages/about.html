Recon Ops

Recon Ops is a fast paced fps shooter. Play with your friends or play with bots in a load of different modes or maps with a load of different guns or skins. Run down your enemy's with a car or call in an support chopper to hit 'em where it hurts.

Recon Ops's Backstory

Recon Ops began all the way back in mid 2020 right after the Dev finshed Covid 20 CO-OP's beta. The very first version of Recon Ops can be found Here. After that very first version the game was refined a lot untill it resembeled what we have today. The version 0.3 was then realesed early 2021 (Around janurary.) After that the project went cold up until October when the dev began to pick it up again and rebuild it after they found the old files on their PC. Now the game is in its current state and is very activly be-ing worked on.



Who Made Recon Ops?

Recon ops is a game made by an indie developer who started working on it in 2020. This developer is behind Max Inc and has made some other games but as of 27/12/21, recon ops is their only published game. This developer has worked on other projects such as the Covid 20 Co-op (which has gotten a reboot in ue4 read more)
